Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy centers on inclusive, student-centered learning that emphasizes critical thinking, historical inquiry, and respectful discussion. I aim to create a classroom environment where students feel empowered to engage deeply with course material and with one another.

Courses Taught

  • Introduction to History
  • World History

Instructional Strategies

I use a combination of direct instruction, inquiry-based learning, primary-source analysis, and collaborative group work to support diverse learners and multiple learning styles.

Assessment Practices

Assessment includes formative checks for understanding, performance-based tasks, reflective writing, and summative projects aligned with learning objectives.
